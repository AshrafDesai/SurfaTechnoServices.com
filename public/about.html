Company Details

SurfaTech Integrated Solutions is a leading provider of specialized solutions in powder coating, spray gun sales and services, and a variety of related services tailored to meet the unique demands of our customers. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we strive to empower businesses by enhancing their surface treatment processes and product finishes.

Our purpose is to enable our clients to achieve superior results through advanced surface treatment technologies. At SurfaTech, we believe in fostering long-term relationships with our clients, built on transparency, integrity, and mutual success.
